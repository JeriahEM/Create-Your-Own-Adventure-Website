You fight the guards.

With your sword and shield, you battle the guards standing at the front gate... and emerge victorious!

You pull a lever, and the gate lowers, revealing a stone staircase that leads into the castle.

Finally inside, you discover what you have been looking for... The dragon.

Shockingly, however, the dragon begins to speak to you. It says that a wizard is the one who put this curse on it to control her, and needs you to free her!

What will you do now?

Trust the dragon and fight the wizard.

Or

Do not trust the dragon and slay it.

Or

Flee from the situation.
